Privacy Policy for itsmyturn.app

This Privacy Policy outlines how Itsmyturn.app collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users (each, a "User") of the itsmyturn.app website and mobile application (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our Service. The types of information we collect depend on how you interact with our Service.

Category Information Collected (Examples) Method of Collection
Identity/Registration Email address, Encrypted Password, Social login information (Profile photo, Nickname) Provided during signup; Automated upon service use.
Optional Data Phone number (optional), Push notification consent status. User's explicit consent.
Usage Data IP address, Cookies, Service usage history (Music playback history, Log data), Device Information (including Push Notification Token). Automatically collected (via Vercel Analytics, Google Analytics, Capacitor).

2. Purpose of Collecting and Using Information

Your personal information is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To identify members, confirm service usage intent, and handle customer inquiries and complaints.
  • Personalization: To provide customized music recommendations based on analysis of music playback history and user preferences.
  • Marketing & Communication: To send Push Notifications and marketing information (including event details) to users who have consented.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze service usage patterns and performance (including Vercel Speed/Web Analytics data) to enhance the user environment.
  • Security: To prevent fraudulent activity and enforce compliance with legal regulations and our Terms of Service.

3. Data Retention and Usage Period

In principle, your personal information is destroyed immediately after the purpose of collection and use has been achieved.

  • Member Information: Retained until the member withdraws from the Service or until the purpose of collection/use is fulfilled.
  • Legal Obligation: Information required to be preserved under relevant laws (e.g., records related to contracts or withdrawal of offers under the e-commerce law) may be retained for the specified legal period (e.g., 5 years).

4. Disclosure of Personal Information to Third Parties

We may provide your personal information to third parties only with your prior consent, or when necessary for the provision of the Service.

  • Recipients: Google Analytics (for analysis), Firebase Cloud Messaging (for push notifications).
  • Purpose: Service usage analysis, push notification delivery.
  • Items Provided: IP address, cookies, device information, usage history (minimum information required for analysis and notification).

5. User Rights

Users and their legal representatives have the right to view, modify, or withdraw their consent for the collection and use of personal information at any time. This can be done through the in-app settings menu or by contacting our Privacy Officer. Users also have the right to request deletion of their account (withdrawal).
